## Practical Reading For This Project

1. Neural compactor target: compare first against Still, ICAE, IC-Former, KV-Distill, SAC, KVSculpt, and LESS. These define the closest methodological frontier.
2. Training-free baseline suite: H2O, SnapKV, PyramidKV, RocketKV, Compactor, FINCH, FAEDKV, Scissorhands, CaM. Report both LongBench and retrieval/multi-turn stress tests.
3. Accuracy-drop reporting should always include the exact retention/compression condition. A method can be near-lossless at 25%-50% retention but collapse at 5%-10%, and LongBench can hide SCBench-style failures.
4. Separate "memory reduction" from "context-length extension" and from "network/TTFT reduction". SnapKV's 8.2x memory-efficiency headline and CacheGen's TTFT gains are not the same kind of claim as token-level KV memory reduction.
5. For a neural KV compressor, the strongest novelty lane is synthetic KV generation with amortized single-pass compression, adaptive per-layer/per-head budgets, KL/distillation objective, and SCBench/NIAH/RULER evaluation.
